How it works

Three keystrokes. Every idea, together.

01

Copy as usual

Thea silently watches your clipboard. Copy from any app, browser, PDF, or document — nothing changes about how you work.

02

Build your thread

Every copy is silently added to your thread — in order, de-duplicated, no limit on sources. Switch windows freely.

03

Paste everything

Hit ⌘ Shift V to paste your full thread — spaced or line-by-line. Anywhere.
